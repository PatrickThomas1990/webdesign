Bio



Maria Galino was born in Austin,Texas in 1992 and raised in a musical household. Both of his parents were into 80's Country music and her father was a manager for a Country singer.


Maria wrote and sold her frist song at the age of 10.


At age 15 Maria sign with JJ records, were she made two albums with them.



By the age of 21 Maria became one of the biggest name in all of Texas. Were she was ask to go on tour with Joel Price and Walter Price the well know Price Bothers. One of the bigest names in Texas Country Music at that time.
